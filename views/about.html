About Us

Where Math Meets Mastery

The Math Collective is BMSIT's premier competitive mathematics community — built by students, for students.

Our Story

Founded in 2026 at BMS Institute of Technology, Math Collective began as a small group of students who believed that mathematics was more than a course — it was a discipline that demanded passion and competition.

We built a digital arena where members face AI-generated challenges, earn XP for correct solutions, and compete for glory on a live leaderboard. Today we're 239+ members strong and growing.

Our mission is to make competitive math accessible, engaging, and deeply rewarding for every level of student.
