Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Math Collective ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our platform at BMSIT.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you register and use Math Collective:

  • Account data: Name, email address, and password (stored encrypted via Supabase Auth).
  • Usage data: Challenge submissions, XP earned, accuracy rates, and session activity.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and device information collected automatically.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your member account.
  • To display your profile and ranking on the leaderboard.
  • To personalize challenge recommendations.
  • To communicate important platform updates via email.
  • To improve our platform through aggregated analytics.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share limited data with:

  • Supabase — our authentication and database provider.
  • OpenRouter — for AI-generated challenge content (no personal data is shared).

4. Data Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase's infrastructure with AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS in transit. Passwords are never stored in plaintext.

5. Cookies

We use session cookies strictly necessary for authentication. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

6. Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time by emailing us at mathcollective@bmsit.in.

7. Children's Privacy

Math Collective is intended for university students (18+). We do not knowingly collect information from users under 18.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions? Contact us at our contact page or email mathcollective@bmsit.in.
